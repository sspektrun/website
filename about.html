ABOUT

What's Spektrun?

Spektrun is an application for Android mobile devices, created to meet the real needs of Spotify users.

Currently, music listening apps and services such as Spotify, Soundcloud, Deezer or YT Music, show the user an overwhelming amount of advertising while trying to listen to music uninterruptedly.

Applications such as Spotify (the most widely used music application on the planet) even limit the user's own actions on Android, such as not being able to go back to listen to a song again, not being able to change the duration of the song at any time, advertising that cannot be avoided, repetitive and abusive, not being able to choose the song they want...

That's why Spektrun was born out of the frustration of those Spotify users who can't afford to pay Spotify Premium every month, and who want a similar experience.

Spektrun's key features

Spektrun has several strong points, which make it a very robust application for the future. Currently, Spektrun has at least three strong points. Of which, we can highlight the following

  • Full Spotify Song's / Album library access
  • Shazam audio recognition
  • High security level-access
  • Youtube Video Player
  • Sync Song's lyrics
  • No ads

Requirements

Before downloading Spektrun, you may want to know the requirements first which are the following

Recommended requirements

  • OS: Android 8 - 15
  • SPACE: At least 500MB
  • RAM: 1GB
  • Internet connection: Stable
  • Spotify account: Yes
  • Email: Yes

Minimum requirements

  • OS: Android 8 - 15
  • SPACE: At least 100MB
  • RAM: 50GB
  • Internet connection: Stable
  • Spotify account: Yes
  • Email: Yes
