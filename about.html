What's Spektrun?

Spektrun is the music app for Android that everyone has always wanted.

Our app offers the user a wide variety of features similar to the rest of the competition, such as searching for songs, looking at trending songs, saving them to your library and related features. However, what really sets Spektrun apart is its high scalability, as well as its extensive additional features such as the ability to watch videos from famous platforms, as well as being able to search for songs using the audio picked up by the microphone.

Spektrun is currently not available to the public as it is a purely educational project. It also has a Whitelist and banning system to reinforce security and limit user access to the app. It also has a Spektrun One To One system. This means that a user can only log in on one device at a time.

In case you want to know about some of the outstanding features of Spektrun that have not been mentioned here, I encourage you to read on.

Spektrun's key features

Spektrun has several strong points, which make it a very robust application for the future. Currently, Spektrun has at least three strong points. Of which, we can highlight the following

  • Full Spotify Song's / Album library access
  • Shazam audio recognition
  • High security level-access
  • Youtube Video Player
  • Sync Song's lyrics
  • No ads
  • Multi-language support

Requirements

Before downloading Spektrun, you may want to know the requirements first which are the following

Recommended requirements

  • OS: Android 8 - 15
  • SPACE: At least 500MB
  • RAM: 5GB
  • Internet connection: Stable
  • Spotify account: Yes
  • Email: Yes

Minimum requirements

  • OS: Android 8 - 15
  • SPACE: At least 100MB
  • RAM: 1GB
  • Internet connection: Stable
  • Spotify account: Yes
  • Email: Yes
